## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Yes! Plans are billed monthly and you can change at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

### What happens if I exceed my rate limit?
You'll receive `429 Too Many Requests` responses. You can upgrade your plan or wait for the rate limit window to reset (1 minute).

### Is there a free trial for paid plans?
Yes! All paid plans include a **14-day free trial** with full features. No credit card required to start.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) via Stripe. Enterprise plans can use invoicing with ACH/wire transfer.

### Can I self-host Key-Value?
Yes! The service is open source (MIT license) and can be self-hosted. The paid plans are for our managed cloud service with support and SLAs. Enterprise plans include managed on-premises deployment.

### What's your refund policy?
We offer a **30-day money-back guarantee** on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

### Do prices include taxes?
Prices are in USD and exclude sales tax/VAT where applicable. Tax will be calculated at checkout based on your location.

### What about data export and portability?
All plans can export data via the API. Pro and Enterprise plans include enhanced export tools. Your data is yours - no lock-in.

### How is usage calculated?
- **Rate limits**: Measured per minute with a sliding window
- **Payload size**: Measured as the JSON payload size (compressed)
- **Tokens**: Count of unique tokens you've created

### Can I have multiple accounts?
Yes, but we recommend using team features on Pro/Enterprise plans instead. Each account needs a unique email address.
